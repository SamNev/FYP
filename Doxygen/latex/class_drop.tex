\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{class_drop}{Drop}} performs all fluid simulation calculations for the program.

It will update terrain heights, manage the aquisition and deposition of sediment, and form pools where acceptable. Each particle simulation is independant of other particles, but their tracked movement may be used to influence other particle simulation.
